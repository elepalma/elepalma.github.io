The lab is based at King's College London, Denmark Hill — one of the UK's leading academic medical centres, with direct access to King's College Hospital and world-class core facilities.

### What to expect

We run weekly group meetings, regular journal clubs, and one-to-one supervision sessions. We encourage early career researchers to present at national and international conferences and support independent fellowship applications.

### Diversity and inclusion

We are committed to building a team that reflects the diversity of the patients we ultimately serve. Applications from underrepresented groups are actively encouraged.

### How to apply

For informal enquiries about any position, please email the PI directly with a brief introduction and your CV. Formal applications should follow the relevant KCL or funding body process.

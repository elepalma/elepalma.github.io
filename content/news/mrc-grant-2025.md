We are thrilled to announce that the lab has been awarded an **MRC Project Grant** worth £480,000 over three years, to support the next phase of our work on biomarker discovery in metabolic-associated fatty liver disease (MAFLD).

The grant will fund a new postdoctoral researcher and PhD student position, as well as extensive multi-omics profiling of samples from the FLR longitudinal cohort.

Professor Smith said: *"This is fantastic news for the whole lab. It will allow us to move from discovery to validation of the most promising biomarker candidates, and to start building towards a clinically usable test."*

We will be advertising the postdoctoral and PhD positions shortly — watch this space.

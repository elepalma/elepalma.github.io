Our latest paper, *"Single-cell transcriptomics reveals fibrogenic niches in metabolic liver disease"*, is now published in **Nature Communications** and available open access.

The paper describes how we used single-cell RNA sequencing and spatial transcriptomics to identify a novel subpopulation of activated hepatic stellate cells, enriched in pericentral zones of the liver, that appear to drive early fibrogenesis in MAFLD.

Congratulations to lead author David Williams and all co-authors. This represents a major output from our BBSRC-funded single-cell atlas project.
